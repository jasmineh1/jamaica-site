Fun Facts about Jamaica

☕ Blue Mountain Coffee is among the most sought-after in the world.
🎶 Birthplace of reggae — home of Bob Marley.
🏃 Usain Bolt and many world-record sprinters hail from Jamaica.
🌋 Dunn’s River Falls empties directly into the sea — rare travertine falls.
🌊 The Luminous Lagoon glows at night (bioluminescence)!
🍽️ National dish: Ackee & Saltfish — savory and colorful.
